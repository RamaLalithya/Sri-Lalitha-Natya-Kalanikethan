About Us

Sri Lalitha Natya Kalanikethan (SLNK) is a prestigious classical dance institute dedicated to preserving and promoting Indian classical and folk dance forms. Originally established in 1988 as Balaji Natya Kalanikethan, it was later renamed Sri Lalitha Natya Kalanikethan in 1999. Under the visionary guidance of its founder, Guru Duttaluri Babu Balaji, SLNK has been a beacon of excellence in Kuchipudi, Bharatanatyam, and Folk Dance training. With a legacy spanning decades, the institute has trained thousands of students who have showcased their talent on esteemed platforms across India, earning numerous awards and accolades. SLNK continues to uphold the rich traditions of Indian classical dance while inspiring future generations of artists through dedicated training, performances, and cultural contributions.

Guru Duttaluri Babu Balaji

Guru Duttaluri Babu Balaji is a renowned classical dance exponent and the driving force behind Sri Lalitha Natya Kalanikethan. With a deep passion for Kuchipudi, Bharatanatyam, and Folk Dance, he has dedicated his life to nurturing young talents and promoting Indian heritage through dance. His expertise and commitment have not only shaped the careers of numerous students but have also contributed significantly to the growth of classical dance in India. His disciples, carrying forward his legacy, have won prestigious awards and performed on national and international stages. Guru Babu Balaji’s unwavering dedication continues to inspire and elevate the art of dance, making SLNK a revered institution in the field of classical performing arts.
